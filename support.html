How to Use GenCatalog

A complete guide to capturing, organizing, and browsing your AI-generated content across Grok Imagine, Higgsfield, and Digen.

Installation & Setup

Get GenCatalog running in a few minutes.

1
Download & Install the App

Mac: Download the DMG from gencatalog.app, open it, and drag GenCatalog to your Applications folder.

Windows: Download the installer (.exe) from gencatalog.app, run it, and follow the setup steps.

2
Choose Your Storage Folder

On first launch, GenCatalog asks you to select a folder where all media and metadata will be stored. Choose anywhere ‚Äî an external drive works great for large collections.

3
Set Up Video Thumbnails (FFmpeg)

GenCatalog uses FFmpeg to generate video thumbnail previews. During setup, a prompt will appear ‚Äî here's what to expect:

Mac: GenCatalog can install FFmpeg for you automatically. The process takes about a minute and requires no technical steps.

Windows: You'll be directed to a download page to grab the FFmpeg files you need. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete setup.

Image thumbnails work immediately without FFmpeg. This step only affects video previews.

4
Install the Chrome Extension

Install the GenCatalog extension from the Chrome Web Store. Works in any Chrome-based browser (Chrome, Brave, Edge, Arc, and more).

5
Start Saving

GenCatalog lives in your menu bar (Mac) or system tray (Windows). As long as it's running, the extension is active and ready across all supported platforms.

Tip: Set GenCatalog to launch at login so it's always ready ‚Äî Mac: System Settings ‚Üí General ‚Üí Login Items. Windows: Settings ‚Üí Apps ‚Üí Startup.

System Requirements

  • macOS 10.15 (Catalina) or later
  • Apple Silicon (M1/M2/M3/M4) or Intel Mac

Bulk Downloading Your Favorites

1
Open the extension popup on grok.com

Make sure you're logged into your Grok account.

2
Click the Grok tab ‚Üí "Scan Favorites"

GenCatalog scans all your favorites and compares against your existing catalog. You'll see: total favorites / already saved / new to download / previously deleted (skipped automatically).

3
Click "Download All New"

Downloads only items not already in your catalog. Progress bar shows real-time status.

4
Pause & Resume anytime

Pause a bulk download and resume it later. Progress is preserved ‚Äî already-saved items won't be re-downloaded.

Multiple Grok Accounts

GenCatalog supports saving from multiple Grok accounts into a single catalog. Each item is automatically tagged with the account it came from, so you can filter your catalog by account. To save from another account: log out of Grok, log in to your second account, and use the extension as normal.

Smart Sync & Exclusion

When you delete an item through GenCatalog, it's added to a permanent exclusion list and will never be re-downloaded during future bulk scans ‚Äî even if it's still in your Grok favorites.

Always delete through GenCatalog ‚Äî not by removing files in Finder. Only GenCatalog-deleted items are added to the exclusion list.

Trash & Deletion

Deleting an item moves it to Trash first ‚Äî nothing is immediately gone.

Managing Trash

  • Click View Trash in the sidebar to see deleted items with deletion dates
  • Restore any individual item, or Restore All
  • Empty Trash to permanently delete all trashed items
Always delete through GenCatalog ‚Äî not via Finder. Finder deletions break the catalog database and don't add items to the exclusion list, meaning they may reappear in future scans.

Exclusion List

Every item deleted through GenCatalog is permanently added to an exclusion list. It will never be re-downloaded during future bulk scans ‚Äî even if it still exists in your Grok favorites.

Duplicate Detection

1
Click "Remove Duplicates" in the sidebar

GenCatalog scans your entire catalog.

2
Review the count

See how many duplicates were found before confirming.

3
Confirm removal

GenCatalog removes duplicates, keeping the oldest version of each item.

Detection uses two methods: exact URL matching, and platform UUID matching (works even if the URL format changes between captures).

Mobile Access

Your catalog is accessible from any device on your Wi-Fi. GenCatalog shows a QR code in the catalog sidebar ‚Äî scan it with your phone to open your catalog instantly. Or type your Mac's local IP (shown in the sidebar) into your phone's browser followed by :8080.

From Anywhere

Install Tailscale (free) on both your Mac and phone. Once set up, you can access your full catalog from anywhere in the world ‚Äî coffee shop, travel, anywhere ‚Äî as long as your Mac is on and running GenCatalog.

Note: Your Mac needs to stay awake for remote access. Consider disabling sleep in System Settings ‚Üí Battery.

License & Activation

After purchasing, you'll receive a license key by email. To activate: open GenCatalog ‚Üí click the menu bar icon ‚Üí select License ‚Üí enter your key and click Activate. Requires internet for initial activation.

License revalidation happens approximately every 7 days, with a 30-day offline grace period. Your files and catalog data are never affected by license status.
