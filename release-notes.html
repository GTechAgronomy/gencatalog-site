GenCatalog 4.4.0

Published February 28, 2026

Latest

Accurate Timestamps

Your catalog now shows when each image or video was actually created, not when it was downloaded. Previously, bulk downloading favorites could assign today's date to everything.

If you already have a catalog with incorrect dates, no action is needed. Timestamps are corrected automatically in the background as you browse your Grok favorites.

Large Collection Support

Users with very large libraries can now bulk download and sync without the popup freezing. Scanning shows live progress, and Sync New Items exits early once it reaches items already in your catalog.

Live Catalog Refresh

The desktop app now updates automatically when new items are saved, so your newest downloads appear without needing manual refreshes.

Updating

  • Desktop app: Version 4.4.0 is available now. You'll be prompted to update on next launch.
  • Chrome extension: Version 4.3.0 is currently in the Chrome Web Store. Version 4.4.0 is pending review and approval.

Future versions will be added here as they are released.
